Raising future leaders

Since 1966, OB Montessori Center has been at the forefront of progressive education, utilizing the Montessori method with traditional and innovative practices.


Our school provides a nurturing learning environment where students from pre-school to Senior High School develop independence, leadership, creativity, and critical thinking skills.
